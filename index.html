How to Play

  • Press the start button to play
  • You will be presented with a question and 4 answers.
  • Each correct answer is worth 10 points.
  • You will have 20 seconds to answer each question. If the countdown timer hits 0 before you have answered, you get 0 points for that question.
  • You will be asked 10 questions in total
  • Put your rock legend knowledge to the test!
